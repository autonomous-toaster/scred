Pattern Tier System:
- CRITICAL: Passwords, keys (highest priority)
- API_KEYS: Third-party credentials
- INFRASTRUCTURE: K8s tokens, vault, etc.
- SERVICES: Payment processors, etc.
- PATTERNS: Generic patterns (JWT, bearer, etc.)

Default Strategy:
- Redact: CRITICAL, API_KEYS, PATTERNS (safe defaults)
- Exclude: INFRASTRUCTURE, SERVICES (lower priority, more noise)
- Users can override: --redact CRITICAL (only critical)
